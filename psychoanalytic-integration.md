### Explanation from the Sources (My Work)   
The core resolution was the creation of a sequence that establishes the three theorists' concepts as **sequential prerequisites** necessary for psychological generativity. The integration follows a three-step structure:   
### 1. Foundation: The Holding Environment (Winnicott)   
Winnicott provides the **Foundation** and the necessary **Prerequisite (Safety)**.   
- The cascade begins with the **Holding Environment**, which is the non-negotiable prerequisite of psychological safety, represented by **Emotional Scaffolding ($\text{S}\_e$)** in the functional formula.   
- The function of the holding environment is to provide **Ontological Security** and stability, protecting the psyche from impingement and allowing the **True Self** to emerge.   
- If this holding environment fails ($\text{S}\_e = 0$), the entire product of growth collapses, regardless of other factors.   
   
### 2. Mechanism: The Alpha-Function (Bion)   
Bion provides the **Psychic Mechanism (The Engine)**.   
- The **Alpha-Function** is the active, metabolic process that is **enabled by the holding**.   
- It functions as the **Processor** ($\text{P}(\alpha)$), transforming raw, unthinkable experience (**Beta-Elements**) into meaningful, thinkable thoughts (**Alpha-Elements**).   
- This mechanism is sparked by the necessary **frustration** (the $\|\text{D}-\text{C}\|$ gap).   
   
### 3. Motive: The Destiny Drive (Bollas)   
Bollas provides the **Motive Force**.   
- The **Destiny Drive** is the **Motive**, which is the resulting **Agentic Expression** or the innate urge of the **True Self** to articulate its unique potential (idiom).   
- The drive is activated only *after* the alpha-function has successfully metabolized experience.   
   
**The unified sequence is: The Holding Environment (Container) enables the Alpha-Function (Processor) to activate, which in turn allows the Destiny Drive (Motive) to be expressed**. This cohesive sequence forms the core conceptual structure that is then mapped onto the neural networks in the subsequent research.
