# Francisco Varela   
Provides the **Methodological Stance**.    
***Neurophenomenology:*** The scientific research program aimed at bridging the "hard problem" by treating first-person experience (phenomenology) and third-person data (neuroscience) as mutually constraining.    
***Enactivism:*** The view that cognition arises through a dynamic interaction between an acting organism and its environment.

He later founded **Neurophenomenology**, arguing that science cannot ignore lived experience but must rigorously integrate it with biological data.
